Net Tracer




This tool works with nmap and python flask. It scans an ip range for open ports, registration data, and spam/fraud detection all in one place! It also supports searching by open ports, and if you click on the port, it will take you to a list of security vulnerabilites that might be present on that port. I wrote all of this in just 24 hours!
